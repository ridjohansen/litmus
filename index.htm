Who Actually Read My E-mail?

Just because someone opened your e-mail doesn't mean they read it. Litmus can break down your open rate to tell you how many people deleted your e-mail, compared to how many read it.

What E-mail Clients Did They Use?

Litmus tracs the e-mail client usage across your mailing lists. This is critical — how else do you know where the focus your testing efforts? Who's reading your messages on a Blackberry?

Individual-level Tracking

E-mail Analytics allows you to collect data for individual subscribers on your mailing list and export it direct from Litmus.

Forwarding and Printing

Incredible though it may seen, Litmus can even tell you if your subscriber printed or forwarded your message. And we track natural forwards (someone using the 'forward' button in their e-mail client), not those done via a web form. No other tools is able to track this metric.
